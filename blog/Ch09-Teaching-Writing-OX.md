# [영어교육론] Ch.9 Teaching Writing OX 21문항 — 해설 완전 정복

Ch.9 Teaching Writing의 핵심 개념을 OX 문항으로 점검합니다. Product/Process approach 구분부터 writing process 5 stages, pre-writing 3종, teacher feedback 비교, peer feedback 함정, writing performances까지 — 기출 단골 함정 포인트 중심으로 해설합니다.

---

## Q1. Product-oriented Approach — ✅ O

> *"The product-oriented approach focuses on producing an error-free final product with little concern for the processes involved, and teachers primarily give feedback on grammar and mechanics."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Product-oriented approach**</span>는 <span style="color:#3182ce">**error-free final product**</span> 산출에 초점을 두며, 피드백도 <span style="color:#3182ce">**grammar와 mechanics**</span> 중심으로 제공합니다. 올바른 언어 모델을 모방·복사·변형(imitate/copy/transform)하는 방식입니다.

---

## Q2. Process-oriented Approach — ❌ X

> *"The process-oriented approach completely ignores the importance of the final product, caring only about the writing process itself."*

<span style="color:#c53030">**⚠️ 기출 단골 함정.**</span> <span style="color:#3182ce">**Process-oriented approach**</span>는 최종 결과물의 중요성을 **무시하지 않습니다**. 다만 error-free text보다 <span style="color:#319795">**well-organized and effective text**</span>를 선호합니다. 피드백은 content와 organization에 집중하며 learner-centered 방식으로 운영됩니다.

- <span style="color:#c53030">**2009.26**</span>, <span style="color:#c53030">**2013.23**</span>, <span style="color:#c53030">**2014.09**</span>, <span style="color:#c53030">**2019.A.14**</span>, <span style="color:#c53030">**2021.B.6**</span> 출제 영역

---

## Q3. Writing Process — Recursive Stages — ❌ X

> *"The five stages of the writing process — pre-writing, drafting, responding, revising, and editing — must be completed in a strictly sequential and linear order."*

Writing process의 5단계는 <span style="color:#319795">**recursive and cyclical**</span>(순환적) 과정입니다. 어느 단계에서나 이전 단계로 돌아가거나 다시 수행할 수 있습니다.

> *"As it is a recursive and cyclical process, any of these stages can be performed or re-performed at any stage in the writing process."*

⚠️ **"strictly sequential and linear"** → 오답. non-linear 구조가 핵심.

---

## Q4. Freewriting — ✅ O

> *"Freewriting is an activity where students freely write down about a given topic without stopping, without concern for formal correctness, often starting from an opening clause or sentence provided by the teacher."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Freewriting**</span>의 핵심 키워드: <span style="color:#319795">**without stop · without formal correctness**</span>. 교사가 opening clause나 sentence를 제공해 시작을 도울 수 있습니다.

⚠️ **Looping과 혼동 주의**: Looping은 freewriting 후 가장 중요한 문장을 골라 **새 주제**로 삼는 추가 단계가 있습니다.

---

## Q5. Looping — ✅ O

> *"Looping is an activity where students write a short passage freely, select a sentence that conveys the most important idea, and use that sentence as the topic of another piece of writing."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Looping**</span>의 3단계: 자유롭게 짧은 글 작성 → <span style="color:#319795">**가장 중요한 아이디어를 담은 문장 선택**</span> → 그 문장을 <span style="color:#319795">**새 글의 주제**</span>로 삼기. freewriting의 확장 형태로, 아이디어를 loop(순환)하며 점점 좁혀갑니다.

---

## Q6. Clustering — ✅ O

> *"Clustering is a pre-writing activity where students jot down all free associations triggered by a key word to make a visual network linking them."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Clustering**</span>: 핵심 키워드에서 촉발된 <span style="color:#319795">**free associations**</span>을 연결하는 <span style="color:#319795">**visual network**</span>를 만드는 pre-writing 활동. 마인드맵과 유사하지만, **Mind map**은 중심 주제에서 아이디어를 발전(develop)시키며 확장하는 점이 차이입니다.

---

## Q7. Drafting — Focus on Accuracy — ❌ X

> *"In the drafting stage of the writing process, students should focus primarily on accuracy and grammatical correctness to produce a polished first draft."*

Drafting 단계에서는 <span style="color:#319795">**fluency and delivery of meaning**</span>에 초점을 두어야 합니다. 학생은 audience(독자), expectation(기대), purpose(목적)를 의식하면서 **의미 전달**에 집중해야 합니다.

⚠️ **정확성은 Editing 단계의 몫**입니다. Drafting에서 accuracy에 집착하면 아이디어 흐름이 막힙니다.

| Drafting | Editing |
|---|---|
| fluency · meaning 전달 · audience 의식 | grammar · spelling · punctuation · diction |

---

## Q8. Formative vs Summative Feedback — ✅ O

> *"Formative feedback aims at encouraging the development of writing by helping students see what they need to do next, while summative feedback is provided at the end of an instructional unit for evaluation."*

- <span style="color:#3182ce">**Formative feedback**</span>: 학습 **과정 중** 제공. 다음 단계 발전을 돕는 ongoing feedback. Process-oriented approach가 강조.
- <span style="color:#3182ce">**Summative feedback**</span>: 수업 단위 **종료 후** 평가 목적으로 제공.

---

## Q9. ECC (Essay Correction Code) — ❌ X

> *"The Essay Correction Code (ECC) is effective for giving feedback not only on language accuracy issues but also on content and organization of students' writing."*

<span style="color:#c53030">**⚠️ ECC 핵심 단점**: "Cannot give feedback on issues about content or organization."</span>

ECC는 language accuracy 문제는 지적할 수 있지만, **content나 organization에 대한 피드백은 줄 수 없습니다**. 또한 errors의 how와 why에 대한 정보도 제공하지 못하며, 학생이 기호를 해독하지 못하면 무용지물이 됩니다.

- 장점: 선별적 언어 오류 지적 · 자기교정(self-correction) 유도 · less threatening
- <span style="color:#c53030">단점: content/org 피드백 불가 · 기호 해독 실패 시 무용 · how/why 정보 없음</span>

- <span style="color:#c53030">**2013.24**</span>, <span style="color:#c53030">**2010.2차논술**</span> 출제

---

## Q10. Conferencing — ✅ O

> *"Conferencing provides opportunities for the teacher and student to negotiate the meaning of the student's writing through discussion, but one drawback is that students may passively incorporate the teacher's suggestions."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Conferencing**</span>의 장단점:

- **장점**: <span style="color:#319795">**meaning negotiation**</span> 가능 · clarifies existing ambiguities · less threatening
- **단점**: <span style="color:#c53030">**time-consuming**</span>(시간 소요) · 학생이 T 제안을 <span style="color:#c53030">**수동적으로 수용**</span>할 수 있음

1:1 면담으로 진행 중인 writing(work in progress)에 대해 대화하는 방식. - <span style="color:#c53030">**2013.24**</span> 출제

---

## Q11. Reformulation — ✅ O

> *"Reformulation is a feedback technique that requires students to compare their first draft with a reformulated version written by the teacher in order to notice the gap."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Reformulation**</span>: T가 학생의 1차 초안을 **재작성(reformulate)**한 버전을 제공 → 학생이 원본과 비교 → <span style="color:#319795">**gap 인식(Noticing Hypothesis 연결)**</span>.

> *"Reformulation helps Ss to notice the gap."*

Noticing Hypothesis(Schmidt): 학습자가 자신의 output과 목표 언어 형식 사이의 gap을 인식(notice)해야 intake가 일어남.

---

## Q12. Peer Feedback — 3종 구분 — ✅ O

> *"Peer feedback focuses on content, peer editing focuses on forms, and peer correction involves actual correction of errors."*

| <span style="color:#3182ce">**Peer feedback**</span> | <span style="color:#3182ce">**Peer editing**</span> | <span style="color:#3182ce">**Peer correction**</span> |
|---|---|---|
| **content** 중심 | **form** 중심 | 실제 **error correction** |

- <span style="color:#c53030">**2010.16**</span>, <span style="color:#c53030">**2013.23**</span> 출제

---

## Q13. Peer Feedback — Disadvantages — ❌ X

> *"One key disadvantage of peer feedback is that students tend to give too little positive feedback, making the activity overly critical and discouraging."*

<span style="color:#c53030">**⚠️ 반대입니다. 실제 단점은 too MUCH positive feedback.**</span>

Peer feedback 실제 단점:
- <span style="color:#c53030">**Vague, unhelpful**</span>
- Sentence-level 문제에만 과도한 집중
- <span style="color:#c53030">**Too much positive feedback**</span> (비판을 꺼려 칭찬만 하는 경향)

"너무 적은 positive feedback"은 사실과 반대. "너무 많은 칭찬"이 문제.

---

## Q14. Peer Feedback — Timing — ✅ O

> *"When advising students on peer feedback, teachers should recommend focusing on content in the earlier stages and on grammar and mechanics in the final stages."*

피드백 타이밍 원칙:
- **Earlier stages** → <span style="color:#319795">**content**</span> 중심
- **Final stages** → <span style="color:#319795">**grammar/mechanics**</span> 중심

Process-oriented approach의 피드백 원칙. 초안 단계에서 문법을 고치면 아이디어 발전을 방해합니다.

---

## Q15. Revising vs Editing — ❌ X

> *"Revising addresses local language issues such as grammar and spelling, while editing addresses global issues such as content and organization."*

<span style="color:#c53030">**⚠️ 기출 단골 함정. 반대입니다.**</span>

| | <span style="color:#3182ce">**Revising**</span> | <span style="color:#3182ce">**Editing**</span> |
|---|---|---|
| 수준 | **Global** issues | **Local** language issues |
| 대상 | content · organization | grammar · spelling · punctuation · diction |

기억법: **Re-vise**(다시 봄, 큰 그림) 먼저 → **Edit**(다듬음, 세부) 나중.

---

## Q16. Dictation (Imitative Writing) — ✅ O

> *"Imitative Writing (Dictation) is an activity where the teacher reads a paragraph in short phrase units with a pause after each unit, and students write down exactly what they have heard during the pause."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Dictation**</span>의 핵심: **pause 있음** · 들은 것을 **그대로 받아쓰기**. Grammar와 vocabulary의 collocation 교수에 효과적.

⚠️ **Dicto-comp와 혼동 주의**:

| | Dictation | Dicto-comp |
|---|---|---|
| Pause | ✅ 있음 | ❌ 없음 |
| 방식 | 그대로 받아쓰기 | 내용 기억 후 재구성 |
| 특징 | — | preparatory stage 있음 |

---

## Q17. Dicto-comp — ✅ O

> *"Dicto-comp is different from dictation in that it does not have pauses, but has a preparatory stage where students listen to the passage multiple times."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Dicto-comp**</span>: 100단어 이상 지문을 여러 번 청취 → 내용을 기억 → **재구성**. <span style="color:#319795">**preparatory stage**</span> 존재가 핵심 구분. pause 없음. listening + writing 통합 활동.

---

## Q18. Dialogue Journal — ❌ X

> *"In a Dialogue Journal, students' entries must be graded and corrected by the teacher in order to improve their writing accuracy."*

<span style="color:#c53030">**⚠️ Dialogue Journal의 핵심 원칙 위반.**</span>

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Dialogue Journal**</span>에서 학생의 일지 항목은 <span style="color:#c53030">**graded, corrected, evaluated 되어서는 절대 안 됩니다**</span>. 교사는 authentic reader로서 written comments로 응답합니다.

> *"Students' entry must not be graded, corrected, or evaluated."*

자유로운 자기표현과 authentic communication, 그리고 **self-assessment** 도구로 활용됩니다.

---

## Q19. Group Writing — ✅ O

> *"Group writing can be suggested as a task that adopts process-oriented approach and also incorporates social constructivism, as it involves collaborative learning through peer scaffolding."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Group writing**</span>은 두 이론적 배경을 동시에 구현:

1. <span style="color:#319795">**Process-oriented approach**</span>: 계획·초안·수정 과정을 함께 공유
2. <span style="color:#319795">**Social constructivism**</span> (Vygotsky): peer scaffolding을 통한 협동학습

학생들이 함께 계획 → 각자 단락 작성 → 상호 검토 → 재작성 → 하나의 글로 조립.

---

## Q20. Real Writing vs Display Writing — ❌ X

> *"Display writing is used for genuine communication of messages to an audience who genuinely wants information, while real writing is used to display one's knowledge in evaluation contexts."*

<span style="color:#c53030">**⚠️ 반대입니다.**</span>

- <span style="color:#319795">**Real writing**</span>: 독자가 답을 모르고 진짜로 알고 싶어하는 **진짜 소통** (편지, 신문 기고, 학술 보고서)
- <span style="color:#c53030">**Display writing**</span>: 지식을 보여주기 위한, 평가가 수반되는 쓰기 (시험 에세이, 교사에게 제출하는 리포트)

⚠️ **시험 에세이 = display writing** (교사는 이미 답을 알고 있음)

---

## Q21. Contrastive Rhetoric — ✅ O

> *"Contrastive rhetoric argues that culturally determined predispositions to structure and organize a text from the first language transfer themselves to second language writing."*

<span style="color:#3182ce">**Contrastive rhetoric**</span>: L1의 **문화적으로 결정된 텍스트 조직 패턴**이 L2 쓰기에 전이된다는 이론. Robert Kaplan 제안.

- 영어: 직선형 / 동아시아 언어: 간접적 / 아랍어: 나선형
- 함의: <span style="color:#319795">**cultural awareness 교육**</span> 필요

> *"this may involve cultural awareness."*

- <span style="color:#c53030">**2012.25**</span> 출제 (product approach + contrastive rhetoric 복합 문항)
